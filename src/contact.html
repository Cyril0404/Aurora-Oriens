FAQ

Common Questions

What materials do you use? +
We use 18K gold-fill, 14K gold-fill, genuine freshwater pearls (AAA grade), nephrite jade, sterling silver, and cubic zirconia set in 24K gold-plated brass. All gemstones are GIA or IGI certified. Every material is nickel-free and hypoallergenic.
Do you offer international shipping? +
Yes — we ship worldwide via DHL Express with full insurance. All orders over $35 qualify for free shipping. Typical delivery is 7–12 business days. Express options are available at checkout.
What is your return policy? +
We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. Items must be unworn and in original packaging. Bespoke and personalized items are final sale. To initiate a return, email us at hello@auroraoriens.com with your order number.
How do I find my ring size? +
Our adjustable rings fit US sizes 5–9. For a precise measurement, we recommend using a ring sizing tool or visiting a local jeweler. If you already know your size in millimeters or another country standard, refer to our size guide or contact us — we're happy to help.
Can I customize a piece from your collection? +
Yes. Our Custom Initials Pendant starts at $150 and includes your choice of initials or word, gemstone center, and chain length. For more involved customizations or heritage replicas, contact us with your concept — we typically respond within 2 business days.
How long does bespoke or custom work take? +
Custom pieces: 5–7 business days. Heritage replicas: 4–8 weeks depending on complexity. Engagement ring design: 3 rounds of sketches, then 4–6 weeks after your final approval. All timelines are discussed upfront before work begins.
How do I care for my jewelry? +
General rule: put jewelry on last when dressing, remove first when undressing. Avoid perfume, moisturizer, and water. Store in the provided pouch or a separate compartment. Jade is the most durable — clean with warm water and a soft brush. Pearls should be wiped after wearing. Gold-fill can be polished with a jewelry cloth.
Are your pieces certified? +
Yes. All jade pieces include a GIA-certified appraisal. Pearls are graded by the Pearl Science Laboratory of China. Enamel and metal pieces carry an IGI certification. Each piece also passes our internal 12-point quality inspection before shipping.
